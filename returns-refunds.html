Returns-Refunds

1. Return Policy

Products can be returned within 7 days of delivery if they are unused, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags attached.

2. Non-Returnable Items

Innerwear, personal care items, and products marked as "Final Sale" are not eligible for returns.

3. Exchange Policy

If you receive the wrong size or a defective product, you may request an exchange, subject to product availability.

4. Refund Process

Once the returned product is received and inspected, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

5. Damaged or Incorrect Products

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact our customer support within 48 hours of delivery with your order details.

6. Need Assistance?

For any questions regarding returns or refunds, contact us at shop@familyfashion.com or call +91 98000 55555.
