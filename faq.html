Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I place an order?

Select your favorite products, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout.

2. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI, Credit Cards, Debit Cards, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery (where available).

3. How can I track my order?

After placing your order, you can track it from the Orders section in your account.

4. Can I cancel my order?

Yes. Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped.

5. How long does delivery take?

Orders are usually delivered within 3–7 business days.
