Shopping Policy

1. Placing an Order

Browse our collections, add your favorite products to the cart, and proceed to checkout to place your order.

2. Payment Options

We accept UPI, Credit Cards, Debit Cards, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery (where available).

3. Order Confirmation

After your order is placed successfully, you will receive an order confirmation via email.

4. Shipping

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days and delivered within 3–7 business days, depending on your location.

5. Order Tracking

You can track your order status from the Orders section after logging into your account.

6. Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped. Once shipped, cancellation may not be possible.

7. Customer Support

If you need any assistance, contact us at shop@familyfashion.com or call +91 98000 55555.
